Frequently Asked Questions

Why choose Thirty Packs of Instant Noodles?

Thirty Packs of Instant Noodles offers the most unique kinds of dishes filled to the brim with all different kinds of flavours created and crafted by our top-tier staff and led by our esteemed head chef Mr Jun.

How consistent is the food?

Our dishes are rigorously tested and refined to ensure every plate meets our gold standard. Whether you're visiting our original location or a franchise, our commitment to consistency remains uncompromised.

Has the restaurant won any awards, received media recognition, or maintained exceptional reviews?

The restaurant has indeed won many awards including three Michelin stars, received a complete 5-star rating and acclaimed criticism, and has maintained it for 7 years and counting. The restaurant is still known as the No. 1 restaurant in the world.

Is the staff well trained and mannered?

Yes, our staff is well trained and mannered. We only hire from the top 5 most prestigious culinary schools for our chefs such as the Culinary Institute of America and Le Cordon Bleu. Our waiters are required to have a graduate degree and over 1000 hours of experience. Even our cashiers and janitors are university graduates. Other roles require Ivy League education along with culinary degrees.

How fresh and locally sourced are the ingredients used?

Our ingredients are completely fresh as we only use produce that is less than 16 hours old. We never source ingredients from places over 5 km away from our franchises. The restaurant also uses only organic ingredients to ensure customer health and wellbeing.
