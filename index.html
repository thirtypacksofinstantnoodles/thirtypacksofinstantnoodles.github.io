Thirty Packs of Instant Noodles: A Journey


Our Past:

Our esteemed founder JJ Pang is also known as a billionaire, philanthropist, celebrity, businessman, and president of the esteemed michelin company. Since he was a child, he grew a fond taste for instant noodles which ignited his passion to make instant noodles become a dish that could be favoured by everyone. During the past few years, JJ Pang created multiple inventions and techniques which all helped make the dish instant noodles become the number one selling dish, due to its cheap price and savoury taste. The restaurant Thirty Packs of Instant Noodles has now been opened in over a 1000 locations and has received acclaimed criticism.


Why choose Thirty Packs of Instant Noodles?

Thirty packs of instant noodles offers the most unique kinds of dishes filled to the brim with all different kinds of flavours created and crafted by our top-tier staff and led by our esteemed head chef Mr Jun.

How consistent is the food?

The food's quality is guaranteed to be consistent and is indeed proven through a survey done by food critics all around the world.

Has the restaurant won any awards, received media recognition, or maintained exceptional reviews?

The restaurant has indeed won many awards including three Michelin stars, received a complete 5 star rating and acclaimed criticism and has maintained it for 7 years and counting, the restaurant was and is still known as the no.1 restaurant in the world.

Is the staff well trained and mannered?

Yes our staff is indeed well trained and mannered, as we only hire from the top 5 most prestigious culinary schools for our chefs such as culinary institute of america and Le cordon Bleu, for our waiters they have to have a graduate degree from a university and have at least 1000 hours of experience, our cashiers and janitors all also have graduated from university. Other roles require a graduate degree from an ivy league school along with a graduate degree from the culinary institute of america.

How fresh and how fresh and locally sourced are the ingredients used?

Our ingredients are completely fresh as we only use produce that is less than 16 hours old, we also never source ingredients from places over 5 km far from our franchises. The restaurant also only uses organic ingredients which helps ensure the customer's health and wellbeing.
