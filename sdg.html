We provide employment opportunities for chefs, servers, cleaners, delivery staff, and more. We offer jobs to youth, women, elderly workers, or marginalized groups, helping ensure equal access to economic opportunities.

Our franchise taxes customers who waste food. For example, for every 100g of food wastage, we tax the customers RM10. This helps encourage customers to take away food or to finish their food to prevent being taxed. We take left over food and provide them to farmers. Farmers can use these food wastage and feed them to the pigs. The pig manure is then used as compost. We also keep some of the more moldy food in a storage bin, as pigs are unable to eat moldy food. The moldy food is kept until there are no more storage space, then it's grinded into a composter. The grinded food is given to farmers to use as compost.
